张纯如纪念馆

· 2019-12-11 · # 记录

1 概览

Iris Chang Memorial Hall

图片上的纪念馆是国际国内首家全景式展示美籍华人、淮安之子、著名历史学家、人权斗士张纯如一生业绩的专题馆。纪念馆坐落于风光旖旎的古淮河北岸，背倚淮阴大地，面向日出东方，庄重典雅，朴素沉静。她以勿忘国耻、珍爱和平、怀念英雄、励志后人为宗旨，以“不能忘却的纪念”为情感纽带，设计布局，布置展陈。

The memorial hall in the picture is the first panoramic exhibition of Chinese Americans, the daughter of Huai’an, a famous historian and human rights fighter Iris Chang. The memorial hall is located on the north bank of the ancient Huaihe River with beautiful scenery, leaning against the earth of Huaiyin, facing the sunrise east, solemn and elegant, simple and quiet. She does not forget national shame, cherishes peace, misses heroine, motivates future generations as the purpose, takes "unforgettable memory" as the emotional link, designs the layout, arranges the exhibition .

2 展厅

走进展厅，呈现在人们眼前的展馆，布展以蓝紫色为主基调，以张纯如写作《南京大屠杀》为核心，阐发其作品和精神给世界和人们带来的巨大影响与启迪。

Enter the exhibition hall and present the exhibition hall in front of people. The layout of the exhibition is based on blue and purple, with Iris Chang's The Rape of Nanking as the core, expounding the great influence and Enlightenment of his works and spirit to the world and people.

3.张纯如简介

张纯如（1968年3月28日－2004年11月9日），华裔女作家、历史学家，祖籍江苏淮安，生于美国新泽西。以出版英文历史著作《南京大屠杀》（The Rape of Nanking）而闻名。

Zhang Chunru (March 28, 1968 - November 9, 2004), a Chinese woman writer and historian, was born in New Jersey, USA. Her ancestral home is Huai'an.
She is famous for her English historical work The Rape of Nanking.

4.《南京暴行》

1997年出版的《南京暴行》在一个月内就打入美国最受重视的《纽约时报》畅销书排行榜，并被评为“年度最受读者喜爱的书籍”。美国《新闻周刊》对这本书的评论是：对二战中最令人发指的一幕作了果敢的回顾，改变了所有英语国家都没有南京大屠杀这一历史事件详细记载的状况。该书曾连续14周名列《纽约时报》畅销书排行榜，至今已经再版十余次，印刷近100万册。

The Rape of Nanking, published in 1997, entered the best-selling New York Times bestseller list in the United States within one month, and was named "The Book Most Popular by Readers of the Year." The US Newsweek commented on the book as a bold review of the most heinous scenes of World War II, which changed the situation in which all English-speaking countries did not have a detailed record of the Nanjing Massacre. The book has been ranked in the New York Times bestseller list for 14 consecutive weeks, and has been reprinted more than ten times, with nearly 1 million copies printed.

5.QA

Q: 为何要在淮安建张纯如纪念馆？
Why build Iris Chang Memorial Hall in Huai'an?
A: 记者连线了张纯如母亲，并到淮安实地采访，得知张纯如祖籍是淮安，建馆是以家乡的名义，纪念这位杰出的华裔作家、淮安女儿。

The reporter connected with Iris' mother and interviewed on the spot in Huai'an, and learned that Iris Chang's ancestral home was Huai'an, and the building was in the name of her hometown to commemorate this outstanding Chinese writer.

6.历史背景

她的祖籍在淮安，是家乡人民的骄傲。为了更好地表达家乡人民对她的敬仰之情，2015年，淮安市侨联提议建设张纯如纪念馆，同年5月张纯如纪念馆筹建工作开始启动。淮安市委书记姚晓东十分重视，将其列入专项研究。江苏省社科院研究员孙宅巍认为，在淮安建成张纯如纪念馆，弘扬她求实的精神，反映了故乡人民对她怀有的深厚感情，对她卓越贡献的充分认可。

Her ancestral home in Huai'an is the pride of the people in her hometown. In order to better express the hometown people's admiration for her, in 2015, Huai'an Federation of overseas Chinese proposed the construction of Iris Chang Memorial Hall. In May of the same year, the preparation for the construction of Iris Chang memorial hall began. Yao Xiaodong, Secretary of Huai'an Municipal Party committee, attached great importance to it and included it in the special study. Sun zhaiwei, a researcher at Jiangsu Academy of Social Sciences, believes that the construction of Zhang Chunru Memorial Hall in Huai'an will promote her pragmatic spirit, reflect the deep feelings of the people of her hometown and fully recognize her outstanding contributions.
