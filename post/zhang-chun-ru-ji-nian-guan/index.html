1 张纯如纪念馆

Iris Chang Memorial Hall
![张纯如纪念馆](https://545646733.github.io//post-images/1576073278107.png) 图片上的纪念馆是国际国内首家全景式展示美籍华人、淮安之子、著名历史学家、人权斗士张纯如一生业绩的专题馆。纪念馆坐落于风光旖旎的古淮河北岸，背倚淮阴大地，面向日出东方，庄重典雅，朴素沉静。她以勿忘国耻、珍爱和平、怀念英雄、励志后人为宗旨，以“不能忘却的纪念”为情感纽带，设计布局，布置展陈。 The memorial hall in the picture is the first panoramic exhibition of Chinese Americans, the daughter of Huai’an, a famous historian and human rights fighter Iris Chang. The memorial hall is located on the north bank of the ancient Huaihe River with beautiful scenery, leaning against the earth of Huaiyin, facing the sunrise east, solemn and elegant, simple and quiet. She does not forget national shame, cherishes peace, misses heroine, motivates future generations as the purpose, takes "unforgettable memory" as the emotional link, designs the layout, arranges the exhibition .
